A Dispatch Operator, or dispatcher for short, is a key position in a logistics company. Dispatchers manage their fleet of truck drivers. Truck Maintenance Operator

On Site

/

English B2+

A Truck Maintenance Operator is the point person for coordinating breakdown requests from truck drivers. In short, when a truck breaks down in the middle of the road, the operator connects our drivers with the most relevant truck repair shops. They make sure our fleet of trucks stays in peak condition. On a typical day, you would conduct daily phone conversations with drivers and repair facilities, manage breakdown requests from drivers and coordinate repairs with our trusted partners and schedule routine maintenance procedures and inspections.
